Orange Marmalade Burrito

Ingredients:

  • Flour Tortilla
  • Orange Marmalade
  • Whipped Cream

How to make it:

  1. Warm a tortilla in the microwave.
  2. Spread a very thin layer of orange marmalade all over one side of it.
  3. Spray a thick strip of whipped cream along it.
  4. Roll the tortilla in a tube.
  5. Eat.
